Thai Curry

Description

mmM curry mmMm

Ingredients

  • Rice: 1 and 1/4 cups brown jasmine rice or long-grain brown rice, rinsed
  • Oil: 1 tablespoon coconut oil or olive oil
  • Onion: 1 cup of chopped white onion
  • Ginger: one tablespoon of grated ginger
  • Garlic: 2 cloves of garlic, pressed or minced
  • Seasonings: pinch of salt and sugar
  • Bell Pepper: 1 yellow, orange, or green bell pepper, sliced into 2 inch long strips
  • Coconut Milk: 1 can or 14oz of regular coconut milk
  • Carrots: 3 carrots, peeled and sliced

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cook onion with ginger and garlic
  3. Add bell peppers and carrots
  4. Add coconut milk
  5. Simmer and cook until vegetables are softened to liking over medium heat
