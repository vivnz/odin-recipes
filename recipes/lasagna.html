Lasagna

Description

yummy lasaga :3c

Ingredients

  • Meat: italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic: one whole onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato: can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste
  • Sugar: two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Seasonings: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: store bought or homemade is chill
  • Cheeses: parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg: help binds the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving
